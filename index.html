About Me

Results-driven. Detail-oriented. Operationally excellent.

I am a Warehouse & Supply Chain Specialist with over a decade of hands-on experience in warehouse operations, materials management, inventory control, and logistics coordination within FMCG and manufacturing environments.

My career spans three leading organisations — Chemical and Allied Products PLC, Suntory Food & Beverage Nigeria Ltd, and GSK Nigeria — where I have consistently delivered accuracy, compliance, and efficiency across the supply chain.

I combine deep expertise in SAP and MFG Pro with a strong commitment to HSE, GMP, and ISO standards, always focused on optimising processes and supporting production targets.
